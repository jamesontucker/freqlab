# Airwindows

Massive collection of production-quality audio effects. MIT License.

## Notable Plugins
- **Console/Density**: Saturation and warmth
- **Pressure/CStrip**: Compression
- **ToTape/IronOxide**: Tape emulation
- **FireAmp/GrindAmp**: Guitar amps
- **DeEss/DeBess**: De-essing
- **ToVinyl/DeRez**: Lo-fi effects

## When to Use
- First choice for most effects
- Saturation, compression, tape, guitar, lo-fi
